Do we pay as much attention to our surroundings
as we want in our daily lives?
There are so many hidden colors which we
pass by without appreciating everyday in our cities.
Let's take a small break from our stressed lives
and start 'color-hunting'.
Can you become the Master Observer of your city?



The Purpose

Through this website project, I wish to make an archive
of 'palettes' of colors from all around the world.



Care to Contribute?

If you wish to share the colors of YOUR city, send a photo
from your color-hunt library to the below email address.
You can leave your trace anonymously on this website :)))
